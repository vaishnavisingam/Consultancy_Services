1. What Are Business Process Sourcing (BPO)Services?

A BPO is a type of outsourcing that involves the contracting of the operation and responsibility of a specific business process to a third-party services provider. A company's BPO means hiring another company for some work. The services include payroll, accounting, telemarketing, data-recording, SMM, and customer support.


2. Why Is BPO Important?

Business process outsourcing (BPO) allows you to focus on the main mission of your business. Companies hire outside agencies for routine "non-core" actions such as email support, bookkeeping, or accounting. One industry that uniquely benefits from BPO is the healthcare industry. It gives time to concentrate on important matters.
