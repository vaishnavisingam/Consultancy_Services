Payroll service is a term used to define a company’s record of its employee’s salaries, wages, and bonus along with the taxes withheld. Payroll service in a real sense is an account or record of the money spent on employee salary, wages, and taxes for hiring the employee services. The maintenance of records concerned with payroll is mandatory for companies in compliance with the provisions of income tax and other laws of the land.
